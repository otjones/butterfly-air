Inspired by nature

When butterflies are present, we know the air is healthy.

Our purpose is deeply rooted in nature. Butterflies are sensitive indicators of the health of our environment. Scientific studies have proven the correlation between the presence of butterflies and healthy air. Air pollution obscures the smell of flowers, making it much harder for pollinators to find them. Therefore, when butterflies are present it’s a sign of clean, healthy air.

Air matters. Today more than ever.

Read the latest report from Imperial College

Researchers are beginning to understand how air pollution can affect the brain and the mind.

A recent report released by Imperial College London highlighted the lifelong impact of air pollution. It may look like the air is empty, but that's far from the truth. 1 m³ of air weighs 1.2kg and contains 1 trillion molecules and atoms. If the concentrations of gases and particles in this mixture of air is not right then it can really affect our health, wellbeing and cognitive ability. So it's important to have accurate information that can help us make good decisions.

Knowledge is Power.

Accurate information is our Mission.

Butterfly is the right tool to ensure you are accurately informed to make better decisions about the quality of air in your space. Your Butterfly air companion.

Protect your loved ones. By having access to data on indoor air quality, you can identify potential health risks and take appropriate measures to protect yourself and your loved ones. With the help of the Butterfly App, you can analyse the trends in your space, allowing you to identify patterns that may have long-term health effects.

Personalised tips. The more you engage with your Butterfly, the more you’ll receive personalised suggestions and useful information on your indoor air quality (IAQ). This will include valuable insights about the air you breathe every day, helping you become more aware of potential pollutants. As a result, your understanding of the factors that may be affecting your indoor environment will continue to expand.

Timely decisions. Butterfly's real-time data will promptly help you detect sudden changes and take immediate, appropriate actions. With this information at hand, you will be able to make informed decisions on how to optimize your space. This could involve strategically adjusting ventilation, maintaining suitable humidity levels, or making changes to your cleaning habits and lifestyle as necessary.

Super-power your home. When the app is connected to smart assistants like Alexa, you gain greater control over other IoT devices such as dehumidifiers, air conditioners, and heaters. This allows you to optimize your indoor air quality (IAQ) while minimizing your energy bills.
